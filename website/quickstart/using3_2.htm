Navigation and Management of Map Preview The software displays a preview of the map in the center of the screen. You are able to move the map as well as zoom in and out. To do so you can use the mouse or keyboard of your computer.  To enlarge the visible area you can deactivate the panels by using the menu bar ("Panels").
